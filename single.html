Philippine Army Officer's Ladies Foundation School Celebrates 50th Foundation Day

Admin

Web Design

15

Manila, Philippines – The Philippine Army Officer's Ladies Foundation School (PAOLFS) celebrated its golden jubilee with a grand 50th Foundation Day celebration held on November 27, 2024. The milestone event brought together students, faculty, alumni, and distinguished guests to honor the school’s five decades of excellence in education and service.

The celebration kicked off with a colorful parade showcasing the school’s history and achievements, followed by a thanksgiving mass. Highlights of the event included cultural performances by students, an inspiring speech from a notable alumna, and the unveiling of a commemorative plaque marking the school’s 50 years.

“This is not just a celebration of our past, but also a commitment to continue building a brighter future for our students,” said PAOLFS Principal, Mrs. Elena Santos.

The event also featured a time capsule ceremony, where letters and memorabilia were buried to be opened on the school’s 75th anniversary. Guests enjoyed a festive program filled with music, dance, and a heartwarming tribute to the school’s founders.

Its 50th Foundation Day celebration was a testament to its enduring legacy and bright future.

The Philippine Army Officer's Ladies Foundation School was established in 1974 to provide quality education for the children of military families and has since become a beacon of learning and community spirit.
